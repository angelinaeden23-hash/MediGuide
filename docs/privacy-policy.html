MediGuide Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Overview

MediGuide is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app works and what data we do and do not collect.

2. Information We Collect

MediGuide does not collect personal information. The app works entirely on your device. Any information you enter (such as app settings or quiz progress) is stored locally or in your personal account and is never shared with us or any third party.

3. Location

Location is accessed only when you explicitly tap "Find Nearby Hospitals." It is used solely to show nearby emergency facilities and is never stored, logged, or transmitted to our servers.

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use advertising IDs or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share data with third-party advertisers
  • We do not access location in the background
  • We do not use geofencing

5. Children's Privacy

MediGuide includes a Child Mode for younger users. We do not collect any personal information from users of any age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
